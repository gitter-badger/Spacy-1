\subsection{Detailed Description}
Creator for \hyperlink{classSpacy_1_1ProductSpace_1_1Vector}{Product\+Space\+::\+Vector}. 

Represents a product space $X = \{X_0,X_1,\ldots,X_n\}$. This structure may be further restricted by specifying if variables act as \char`\"{}primal\char`\"{} or \char`\"{}dual\char`\"{} variables. Then we get $X=\{ X_p, X_d \}$, where $X_p=\{X_i\}_i,\ i\in I $ is associated to the primal variables and $X_d=\{X_j\}_j,\ j\in J$ is associated to the dual variables.
