Detailed Description

Creator for ProductSpace::Vector.

Represents a product space \(X = \{X_0,X_1,\ldots,X_n\}\). This structure may be further restricted by specifying if variables act as "primal" or "dual" variables. Then we get \(X=\{ X_p, X_d \}\), where \(X_p=\{X_i\}_i,\ i\in I \) is associated to the primal variables and \(X_d=\{X_j\}_j,\ j\in J\) is associated to the dual variables.
