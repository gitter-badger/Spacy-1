Detailed Description

The affine covariant step method described in [5], [4] for the solution of equality constraint optimization problems.

An affine covariant composite step method for the solution of problems of the form

\[\min f(x)\quad \text{s.t.}\quad c(x)=0\]

, based on the corresponding Lagrange functional

\[L(x,p) = f(x)+pc(x)\]

.
